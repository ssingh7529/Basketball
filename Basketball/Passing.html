Essentials of Passing

    
  
  
    

Passing is a critical component of a basketball game that requires agility, speed and a deep-seated awareness of the ball, your teammates, and opponents. Moreover, passing is multi-dimensional in nature and is only as effective to the extent that it is utilized correctly on the basketball court at any given moment in time. Consequently, simplicity is usually the best strategy to use, however, simplicity requires patience and practice and passing only becomes effective when utilized at exactly the right moment at the right time on the basketball court.
